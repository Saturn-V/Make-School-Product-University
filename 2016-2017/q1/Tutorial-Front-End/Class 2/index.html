Nasa Style Guide

Nasa Style Guide is an easy and effective way to practice Nasa's style guidelines. It's a great way to improve your ability and appreciation of design.

Using the Nasa Style Guide app you can practice styles anywhere you are, while also enjoying some sexy design guidelines that make Nasa have a retro vibe.

How to use the app

Select the page you want, and look at the dope styling. Pick up on the guidelines fast, and show off your 10/10 design techniques.

For beginners start with three or four easy designs like:

  • Text Positioning
  • Logo Positioning
  • Information Presentation

When you can confidently identify these add a new page to your practice set.
